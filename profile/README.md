**MOB4AI** is a **Research, Development and Innovation (R&D&I)** project focused on advanced **Artificial Intelligence for mobile and embedded systems**, with emphasis on **Neuromorphic Computing**, **Mobile Networks**, **Large Language Models (LLMs)** and **energy-efficient optimization** for smartphones and neuromorphic hardware.

The project develops applied research and software solutions for **Motorola**, targeting energy efficiency, performance optimization and improved quality of mobile services.

## üéØ Research Scope

MOB4AI investigates the relationship between **mobile network quality** and **energy consumption** in mobile devices, exploring AI-driven and neuromorphic approaches to optimize embedded systems.

Main research topics include:

- Neuromorphic Computing and **Spiking Neural Networks (SNNs)**
- Energy efficiency in **mobile and embedded systems**
- Mobile networks and quality-of-service analysis
- **On-device AI** and system-level optimization
- **LLMs for data analysis and modeling**
- Neuromorphic hardware (e.g., **Xylo Audio 3**)
